About us Raskbind

Raskbind Coffee embarked on its remarkable journey on January 1, 2023, with a team of visionary individuals at its helm. The founder of the company is Rahul Sharma, a true trailblazer in the coffee industry. Assisting Rahul in shaping the company's success is Aryan Mishra, the co-founder, who brings his expertise and passion for coffee to the table. Leading the company as the CEO is Hami Aly, whose leadership skills and strategic vision have propelled Raskbind Coffee to new heights. Together, this dynamic trio has created a brand that prides itself on providing premium-class coffee to its esteemed customers. With their collective dedication, Raskbind has garnered a reputation for excellence, offering only the finest coffee products that have captivated the taste buds of coffee lovers worldwide. As the journey continues, Raskbind Coffee remains committed to delivering a remarkable coffee experience under the guidance of its visionary founders.
